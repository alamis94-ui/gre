## Garden Description

A funerary plaque (evidently complete) discovered in 1861 beside the Via Praenestina near Tor Sapienza records the dimensions of a tomb plot "with a garden enclosed by a wall" eighty-one and a half feet in frontage, forty-three and a half feet in depth (ca. 320 square meters).
