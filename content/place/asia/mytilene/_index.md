## Place Description 

The city of Mytilene was located on the island of Lesbos lying off the west coast of Asia Minor.  Gardens, vineyards and olive groves in the territory of Mytilene are known from a list on a Roman marble inscription that has survived in several fragments (IG XII.2 76-80). <!--these are the first two sentences of "Rural Estates.md"-->
