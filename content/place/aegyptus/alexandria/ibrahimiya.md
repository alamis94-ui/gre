## Garden Description

Ibrahimiya was an eastern suburban necropolis of Alexandria. Size not given. Constructed in the 1st century CE by Pomponia Mousa for her son, husband, freedmen and freedwomen; object of litigation before the Idios Logos ca. 90 CE. No information given about what was planted in it; a *καρπιστής* mentioned is probably owner of rights to harvest the produce.
