## Garden Description

One-half aroura (.14 ha.). Leased in 69 CE for 3 years. Includes fruit trees of unspecified type (*ἀκρόδρυα*), up which vines are trained; vineyard is described as anadendradic. Requires dung and *ammos* (sand).
