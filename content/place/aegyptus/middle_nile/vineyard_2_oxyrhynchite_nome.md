## Garden Description

A reed plantation, orchard, and garden; location not preserved. Leased in 141 CE for a number of years now lost. Furnished with a sakiya, a small watchtower, and a walled animal enclosure. Requires applications of *χοῦς* and dung. Rent includes cash, wine, fresh grapes, fresh and pressed dry dates, green and black olives, pomegranates, peaches, figs, myxes, two types of gourds, and vegetables (*λάχανα*) according to season.
